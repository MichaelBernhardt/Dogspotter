App Name: DogSpotter
Short Description: The offline field guide for dog lovers. Identify breeds and log sightings.

Full Description:
DogSpotter is your ultimate companion for discovering and documenting the world of dogs. Inspired by bird-watching field guides, DogSpotter lets you build your own "life list" of dog breeds you've encountered.

Whether you're at the park, on a walk, or just curious about a pup you see, DogSpotter helps you identify breeds with its smart trait-based search. Browse our extensive offline library of over 50 dog breeds, complete with photos, traits, and descriptions.
